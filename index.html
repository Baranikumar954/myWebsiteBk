NEOMAT

( An Ultimate Headcount Solution )

NEOMAT is an advanced solution for emergency headcount maintenance, using technologies like Machine Learning and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) alongside Force Sensing Resistors (FSRs) to detect footsteps and provide precise data. It overcomes the limitations of traditional methods like Infrared and Thermal Imaging, offering a safer and more reliable alternative. NEOMAT supports global initiatives, contributing to the UN's SDG 9 (Innovation & Infrastructure) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities) by enhancing emergency preparedness and community resilience through innovative technology.

IntelliQuest

Automated Resume-Based Interview Question Generation Using AI

IntelliQuest is an AI-powered solution designed to automate the generation of interview questions tailored to a candidate's resume. By utilizing advanced natural language processing (NLP) techniques, the system extracts key skills, qualifications, and experiences from resumes and creates relevant, role-specific interview questions. This automation reduces the manual effort required by recruiters, improves the precision of candidate assessments, and ensures a more consistent and objective interview process. IntelliQuest enhances efficiency, enabling a streamlined hiring experience while maintaining a high standard of evaluation.
